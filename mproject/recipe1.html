Salsa Quemada (Roasted Salsa)

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Large Tomatoes (any variety will work)
  • 3-4 Serrano Chilies (you can also use Jalapenos)
  • 1 Onion, peeled and cut in half
  • 1 Bunch of Cilantro (stems removed)
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled (or 1 teaspoon garlic powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup of water

Directions

  1. Place tomatoes, onion, chilies,and garlic on a pan and roast in the oven under the broiler until outsides of veggies are charred, turning over once. Alternatively you can roast on a grill or open flame.
  2. Do not remove charr. Carefully cut stems off chilies, cut in halves (can be de-seeded for less heat), cut onion halves into smaller chunks (to fit easily in blender), cut tomatoes into quarters. Place all charred veggies in blender.
  3. Into blender along with charred veggies, add half of the water, all the cilantro (or to taste), salt (to taste), and if using garlic powder instead of garlic cloves add to blender.
  4. Blend on high until well combined. Taste and adjust as needed. If too spicy, add more tomatoes, if not spicy enough add more chilies.
  5. Serve with tortilla chips, and enjoy. This will keep in the fridge for a week.
