Easiest Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

1.Line a baking tray with parchment paper.

2.Cream together butter and sugar until fluffy.

3.Form dough balls and place on tray.

4.Bake until golden brown. Enjoy!
