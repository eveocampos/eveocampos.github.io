Pacific Rhododendron

About the Pacific Rhododendron

  • The Pacific Rhododendron is the state flower of Washington State.
  • Offical Name: Pacific Rhododendron , Rhododendron macrophyllum
  • Also Known As: Coast Rhododendron , California Rhododendron, or California Rosebay.
  • The Pacific Rhododendron is found west of the Cascades from southern British Columbia to Northern California.
  • The preferred climate of a Pacfic Rhododendron is mild, with mild winters and cool summers away from direct sunlight.
  • This plant can grow to around 16 feet tall and 16 feet wide, most reaching approximately 9 1/2 feet by 10 years old.
