Mexican Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 whole disc of Abuelita Round Chocolate Tablets
  • 4 Cups of Milk of your choice (we use almond milk)

Directions

  1. Abuelita's chocolate discs are very hard, I find it easiest to start by removing paper and microwaving in a bowl for about 30-60 seconds. This will soften the chocolate enough to allow mixing it easily in the milk. (Alternatively, you can just add it to your milk. Just be aware it will take a lot longer to melt and you will have to whisk extra.)
  2. Add milk to sauce pan and heat over medium high heat. Add your chocolate and whisk constantly while milk heats up.
  3. Continue whisking until drink starts to boil and becomes frothy.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Serve (pairs well with Bunuelos) and enjoy!
