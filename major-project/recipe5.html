Easy Ground Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground Beef.
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp Olive oil + more for tortillas.
  • 1 tsp each Garlic powder and Onion powder.
  • Salt and Pepper to taste.
  • 8-10 Corn Tortillas
  • Toppings:shredded lettuce, chopped jalapenos, chopped radishes, chopped cilantro, chopped onions, shredded cheese, salsa (can use the recipe:Salsa Quemada), guacamole (can use the recipe:Guacamole), lime wedges for squeezing, and other toppings your heart desires.

Directions

  1. Start with adding 1/2-1 tsp of Olive oil to pan and heat on medium high heat. Add your ground beef to pan.
  2. Cook until beef is almost done,and drain grease. Then add garlic and onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. While beef is cooking prepare your toppings.
  4. For Tortillas: You can warm them in a non-stick pan (then wrap the stack in foil to keep them warm), or alternatively, you can heat up a bit of oil in a pan and fry the tortillas for about 10-15 seconds on each side (they will be pliable). If you choose to fry the tortillas, be sure to lay tortillas on a paper towel, folded in a taco shape to drain. Serve while warm.
  5. Once your beef, tortillas, and toppings are ready: Serve with "Mexican Rice" and "Refried Beans" and enjoy!
