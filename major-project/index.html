A Brief History of Mexican Dishes

Mexican cuisine has its roots in the ancient civilizations of Mesoamerica, including the Olmecs, Maya, and Aztecs. Utilizing ingredients such as maize, cacao, squash, beans, chili peppers, and tomatoes. The Spanish conquest in the 16th century brought new ingredients such as beef, pork, rice, and new fruits and vegetables. This new fusion of foods is known as "mestiza". Over the centuries many cultures have influenced Mexican cuisine from African influences to Middle Eastern. Mexican food has blended these cultures into their own unique cuisine that reflects their resilience and creativity.
