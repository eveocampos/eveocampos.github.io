Refried Beans

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of whole pinto beans with liquid (cooked, undrained) or 2 cans whole pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 Tblsp of butter, divded
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 cup of shredded cheese (mexican blend, cheddar, or your choice)

Directions

  1. To your pan add 1 Tblsp of butter over medium high heat and allow to melt.
  2. To your melted butter add your whole pinto beans and allow to warm through, about 2-3 minutes. (Be careful when adding beans to hot pan, they may splatter a bit)
  3. Once beans are warmed through, take a potato masher and begin to mash your beans. You can mash them as much as you want to (my family prefers to have some beans still slightly whole).
  4. Once beans are mashed to your preference, taste and add salt and remaining butter. Mix well, you can add cheese at this stage if you are adding. Remove from heat and cover with lid to allow cheese to melt.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
