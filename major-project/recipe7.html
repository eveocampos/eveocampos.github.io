Lime Margaritas on the Rocks

Ingredients

  • Glass of choice.
  • Ice
  • 1.5 oz. Tequila
  • 1 oz. Fresh Lime Juice
  • 0.5 oz. Agave Nectar (adjust amount to your sweetness taste preference)
  • For the Garnished Glass Rim: Sea Salt, 1 Lime cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Prepare your glass: Using one wedge of lime, rim the edge of your glass and run the rim in a shallow plate of sea salt.
  2. Place ice, tequila, agave nectar, and lime juice into a cocktail shaker and shake vigorously (if you do not have a cocktail shaker just use two tall glasses: place ingredients into one glass, then top with other and shake).
  3. Add ice to your glass and strain margarita into the glass over the ice.
  4. Sip and enjoy!
  5. Makes 1 cocktail.
