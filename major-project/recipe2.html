Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2-3 ripe Avocados
  • 1-2 Limes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Cilantro, to taste (I use about 1/2 bunch finely chopped)
  • 1/8 tsp - to taste, Garlic Powder
  • 2-3 Tblsp finely chopped Purple Onion (or 1/4 - 1/2 tsp onion powder)
  • Optional Ingredients: 1-2 finely chopped chiles of your choice, 1/2 finely chopped small tomatoe.

Directions

  1. Cut avocados in half and remove seed. Place in a bowl and mash using a potatoe masher or a fork. Then squeeze the juice of one lime over avocado and mix. Set aside.
  2. Chop your cilantro and onion, if using. Then prepare your Optional Ingredients if using.
  3. To your mashed avocado, mix in your chopped ingredients, and start with 1/8 tsp of garlic powder, salt to taste and blend.
  4. Taste and adjust seasonings and ingredients. There is no wrong answer! This is your guacamole.
  5. Serve with tortilla chips, and enjoy.
