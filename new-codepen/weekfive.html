Let's Learn About UX

UX? UI? UXD? UID? UCD? What are all these? Are they parts of web design? Are they the same thing or different things? In these lessons, we start exploring user experience (UX) topics using the 5-level paradigm from the book The Elements of User Experience, Second Edition, by J. J. Garrett. (You are not required to own this book.)

Over readings and videos, we will:

  • get an overview of the 5 levels of UX (as defined by Garrett)
  • learn why we are doing something defines the foundational strategy for the rest of the web design project
  • hear how a clear scope can help design projects
  • look at different types of content organization and information structures for pages and for navigation
  • listen to different UX and UI professionals talk about their fields

There are four lesson videos this week, with a total runtime under two hours. After finishing those, complete the four required Activities this week: three are "quests" and one is an online card sort. There is also an extra-credit Activity.
