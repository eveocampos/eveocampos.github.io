Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Almond Milk
  • 1/4 cup Dairy-Free Butter (Unsalted), softened
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate Chips
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. In a bowl, beat softened butter and sugar until creamy.
  3. Add almond milk and flour. Mix until dough forms.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips evenly.
  5. Scoop dough onto tray with spacing.
  6. Bake 15 minutes or until golden edges.
  7. Cool on tray 10 minutes, then transfer to rack.
